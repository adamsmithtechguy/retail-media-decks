# PRD: Bright.Blue Agency Deck v3

**File:** decks/retail-media-agency-deck-v3.html
**Audience:** Media and retail agencies, first meeting. Warm-up deck: make them love the solution and ask the commercial question themselves. No pricing, margin or commission anywhere.
**Design system:** identical to retail-media-agency-deck.html. Dark navy washdark backgrounds, Space Grotesk white headlines, Inter body, #c7d2ff kickers, #a9c2ff accents. Bright blue wash background reserved for exactly three slides: cover, agency problem, the turn.
**Golden rule:** one idea per slide, minimum text. Every slide is either one big statement, one big visual, or one big number. If a slide needs more than 25 words of body copy, it is wrong (slides 9 and 10 are exempt for tile and chart labels only, not prose). The model slides are the strike-through reveal and the animated map: simple, clear, memorable.

## Narrative arc
Market truth (2) → tension (3) → their pain (4) → punchline (5) → who we are (6-7) → why it's provable (8) → the data product (9-10) → how it fits (11) → how big (12) → proof (13) → close (14).

## Hard rules
- No em dashes anywhere; commas, colons or full stops.
- No slide exceeds 25 words of body copy beyond headline, kicker, stat labels, tile labels, chart labels and footnotes.
- Never the phrases: "screen vendor", "gatekeeper", "Coming soon".
- Backgrounds: wash on slides 1, 4, 5 only; washdark on all others.
- All dashboard numbers internally consistent: interactions < impressions, browses < interactions, conversion consistent with units and impressions.
